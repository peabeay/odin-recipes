Fragrant Thai Green Curry

Delicious Thai Green Curry

Packed with flavour and spice and all things nice, this Thai Green Curry can be made with chicken or in a vegetarian style. Either way you'll love it!

Ingredients

  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Plain Flour
  • Chicken breast, cubed
  • Green curry paste
  • Oniones, chopped
  • Garlic gloves, crushed or chopped
  • Fresh ginger, grated
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar to taste
  • Fish sauce
  • Coriander

Method

  1. Coat chicken in soy sauce, then in flour
  2. Fry chicken until browned, then set aside
  3. In the same pan, heat up curry paste, then add onions, garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant
  4. Add chicken to the pan and stir, coating the chicken in the curry and spice mix
  5. Stir in coconut milk, fish sauce, sugar, some more dark soy sauce then simmer for 20 minutes and until chicken is cooked through
  6. Garnish with coriander

    7. Suggestion:This curry is great with rice!
