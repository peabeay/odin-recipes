Best Lasagna Ever

World's Best Lasagna

This recipe will change everything you thought you knew about lasagna. Perfect for large dinner parties or intimate evenings with friends, everyone will be begging you for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Meat - A combination of beef and pork mince
  • Brown Onion - Diced finely
  • Garlic - Crushed, sliced or minced, as you like
  • Tomatoes - Tomato paste plus fresh tomatoes chopped
  • Sugar - Just a tablespoon
  • Spices - Oregano, Parsley, Basil, Salt and Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles - Store bought is fine!
  • Cheese - Parmesan, mozzarella, or use your favourite!

Method

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese sauce
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest before serving
